About
I am a Ph.D. candidate at IIT Kanpur specializing in Machine Learning, Reinforcement Learning, and Computer Vision, with experience in developing sample-efficient algorithms and advanced control strategies for robotics. My research aims to build intelligent, adaptable systems by integrating deep learning and RL for both simulation and real-world tasks.
